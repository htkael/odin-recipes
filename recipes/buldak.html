Buldak: The Fiery Korean Delight

Description

Buldak, which translates to "fire chicken" in Korean, is a beloved dish known for its intense spice and bold flavors. This Korean classic features tender, bite-sized pieces of chicken marinated and cooked in a fiery gochujang (Korean chili paste) sauce. The marinade typically includes soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and sugar, balancing heat with sweetness for a mouthwatering taste.
